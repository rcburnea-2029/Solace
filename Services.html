Calming Exercises

At Solace, we believe small, simple practices can help you find peace and balance in your daily life. Here are some exercises you can try anytime you feel stressed, anxious, or overwhelmed.

Mindful Journaling

  • Write down your feelings without judgment.
  • Note what triggered them and how your body feels.
  • End with 1–2 positive affirmations or things you're grateful for.

Deep Breathing

  • Inhale slowly for 4 seconds.
  • Hold for 2 seconds.
  • Exhale gently for 6 seconds.
  • Repeat for 5 rounds to relax your mind and body.

Positive Self-Talk

  • Replace negative thoughts with kinder, supportive ones:
  • “I’m doing my best.”
  • “This moment will pass.”
  • “I am stronger than I feel right now.”
